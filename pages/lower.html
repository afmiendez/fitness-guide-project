Leg day

During a leg day, the muscles targeted are the quadriceps, hamstrings and calves. Some exercises involve going up and down (compound movements) and others completely isolate the muscle group targeted. If possible try to complete the exercises in their respective order. A table with all exercises and their respective reps and sets will be available at the bottom of the page

Hack Squat (6-8x4)

Starting the workout with a hack squat is great to target the quadriceps. It is also very effective by being a compound movement which will push your legs to the limit. Aim to complete 6-8 reps and 4 sets and should try to come close to failure during your last 2 reps on the last 2 sets. Rest between 3-4 minutes every set. Watch the following video for a demonstration.

Leg Extensions (8x3)

The leg extensions is also a great way to isolate the quadriceps. It is also very effective at contracting the muscle fibers at the top of the repetition which is crucial for muscle hypertrophy. Aim to complete 8 reps and 3 sets and try to come close to failure during your last 2 reps on the last 2 sets. Rest between 3-4 minutes every set. Watch the following video for a demonstration.

Hamstring Curls (8x3)

The hamstring curls is the perfect hamstrings exercise for a full stretch, control the eccentric while doing the repetitions. Aim to complete 8 reps and 3 sets and try to come close to failure during your last 2 reps on the last 2 sets. Rest between 3-4 minutes every set. Watch the following video for a demonstration.

Calf Raises (8x2)

Now for the final leg exercise, the calf raises is great to target the muscles beloew the knees. Aim to complete 8 reps and 2 sets and try to come close to failure during your last 2 reps on the last 2 sets. Rest between 3-4 minutes every set. Watch the following video for a demonstration.

Tips

  • Always warmup before starting your workouts. It avoids injuries and gets you ready to push yourself even further.
  • If you feel your forearms giving up before your back on a pull day, you can always buy straps to help holding weights.
  • Take your time adjusting to heavy weights, it is not worth risking an injury to try and impress others.

Nutrition

  • Don't forget about hydration throughout the day, with special focus during the workouts
  • Make sure to ingest a diet high in protein, with the recommended amount of daily protein intake (grams) being double your bodyweight in kilograms.
  • Calculate your maintenance calories to see how much do you need to eat daily to gain weight or to lose it.
